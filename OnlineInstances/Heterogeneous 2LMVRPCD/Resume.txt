The source file of testing instances contains four features
1) Capacity:  Capacity of Vehicle, where contains four types of vehicle with different capacity
2) DemandMatrix
3) Instance: the informations of supplier/customer nodes
    ID of node   XCoords   YCoords   Ready time   Due Date   Depot=0 / Supplier=1/Customer =2
4) Rectangle
    Node  XCoords of Items in Vehicle   YCoord of Items in Vehicle   Height of Items   Width of Items
    The XCoords and YCoords of items in vehicle is initialized to zero.
